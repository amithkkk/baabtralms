Cybersquare is the best online platform for kids that offers various online coding courses that will make kids future-ready.It helps kids to expose their creativity and aquire 21st century skills.

Cybersquare learning platform

Every registered student will have a seperate login account to the Cyber square online learning platform. Here, they will be able to view their course progress, assignment provided by teachers, their batch performance, progress cards and so on. They will be able to attend online classes and live classes. Students can attend live classes to clear their doubts and interact with teachers. Apart from this, they will also get notifications pn upcoming live classes, news and events so that they won;t miss them.
